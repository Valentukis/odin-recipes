Kompot

Description

This refreshing drink made by your babushka is a must. If you don't like it, you don't have taste.

Still not sure? No one asked. Just go and make it already.

Ingredients:

  • Fruit
  • Sugar
  • Water
  1. Wash fruits, and pit any stone fruit that you’re using.
  2. Fill a pot with water, and bring it to a boil. Add fruit, and cook uncovered for 5-7 minutes. Switch the heat off, and add sugar to taste. Let the sugar dissolve.
  3. Let the juice come to room temperature, and then either strain it or not (we don’t!). Serve the juice warm (during winter), or chilled!
